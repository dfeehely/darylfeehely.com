About Me

I am a web developer with 12+ years of software development experience. For the past 4+ years I have also project managed medium scale web and mobile based projects. I've worked with startups, SMEs, multinational and semi-state organisations. As a web consultant I help organisations scope and plan projects, lead development teams and build software.

View My CV
Originally from Cork, I went to secondary school in Clare, university in Limerick and now live in Dublin. A childhood of Lego, Atari and Nintendo laid the foundation for my career as a software developer and project manager. I've also developed a keen interest in photography and a fanatical support of Munster Rugby.
